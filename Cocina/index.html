About Festival

An experience that goes beyond your senses

CARIBE VIVO is a unique event where cutting-edge cuisine engages with the biodiversity of the Colombian Caribbean. It is a space to celebrate traditional ingredients, contemporary techniques, and the creative spirit of our region.

Four days of immersive experiences designed for those who view gastronomy as a cultural expression: workshops, tastings, conversations, and signature dinners that redefine what it means to dine in the tropics.
